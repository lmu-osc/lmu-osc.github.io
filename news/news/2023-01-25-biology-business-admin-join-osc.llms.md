# The Faculties of Business Administration and Biology join the OSC

The OSC welcomes these faculties as financially supporting institutional members!

January 25, 2023

The Faculty of Business Administration, represented by [Prof. Dr. Ralf Elsas](../../people/people/ralf-elsas.llms.md "Elsas"), former OSC scientific board member (2017-2023) and the Faculty of Biology, represented by OSC scientific board member [Prof. Dr. Niels Dingemanse](../../people/people/niels-dingemanse.llms.md "Dingemanse") (2023-2025), have joined the OSC as financially contributing institutional members. Researchers from these faculties will have priority access to our training events with a restricted number of seats.
