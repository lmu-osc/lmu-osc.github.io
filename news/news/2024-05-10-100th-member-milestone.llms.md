# The OSC reached 100 individual members!

Announcement

Published

May 10, 2024

With [Dr. Renata Topinkova](../../people/people/renata-topinkova.llms.md "Topinkova"), the OSC now counts 100 individual members. To celebrate this milestone, our 100th member will be offered a free reproducibility check for her next manuscript.

Please consult [the list of all our members](../../people/members.llms.md) to find potential collaborators!

If you’re also interested in becoming an OSC member, you can read more detailed information [here](../../about/join-us.llms.md) and write an email to [malika.ihle@lmu.de](mailto:%20malika.ihle@lmu.de.).
