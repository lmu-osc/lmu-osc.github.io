# New speaker of the OSC’s Scientific Board!

Prof. Dr. Nikolaus Plesnila is the new speaker of the Scientific Board of the OSC

June 26, 2024

The OSC’s Scientific Board now has a new speaker: [Prof. Dr. Nikolaus Plesnila](../../people/people/nikolaus-plesnila.llms.md "Plesnila"). Additionally [Dr. Tobias Straub](../../people/people/tobias-straub.llms.md "Straub") has joined the Scientific Board as [Prof. Dr. Ralf Ludwig](../../people/people/ralf-ludwig.llms.md "Ludwig") has stepped down from the board.

More information about the members of the OSC’s Scientific Board can be found [here](../../people/scientific-board.llms.md "Scientific Board").
