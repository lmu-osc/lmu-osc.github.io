# Open Science Toolbox

Take a look at our new Open Science Toolbox! We offer a collection of helpful links to tools and resources that researchers can use to make their research more transparent and reproducible.

May 16, 2018

How exactly can I make my research more transparent? What software can I use? How do I find Open Access journals? Where do I find practical resources on specific Open Science practices? Where do I find workshop material for teaching Open Science?

In order to close the intention-action gap concerning Open Science practices, we created a collection of resources and tools that can be implemented in a transparent and reproducible research process. As a researcher you can look for resources for every stage of the research process. If you want to teach Open Science or participate in a course yourself, you’ll find examplary workshop material, too.

Take a look at our new [**Open Science Toolbox**](https://osf.io/zjrhu/files/mwng8 "Toolbox") and don’t hesitate to give feedback and suggestions.
