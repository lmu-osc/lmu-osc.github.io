# Stipends for 3 OSC Members for the Open Science Retreat (03.-07.04.2023)

Event Highlights

Published

April 3, 2023

The LMU Open Science Center received 6 applications from LMU members for the Open Science Retreat **organized by [Heidi Seibold](../../people/people/heidi-seibold.llms.md "Seibold")**, and taking place on 3. - 7. April 2023. Three members received a stipend from the OSC to work on creating/fostering an Open Science Initiative at the LMU during the retreat.

The Open Science Retreat is an event from Open Science enthusiast for Open Science enthusiasts. The main goals are:

- Learning
- Getting stuff done
- Networking
- Reflecting
- Rebooting

For more information have a look at the website at <https://open-science-retreat.gitlab.io>
