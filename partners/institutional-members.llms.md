# Institutional Members

The OSC is supported by a growing number of institutional members from across the LMU. Institutional members are key partners in our efforts to promote open science and provide support to researchers at the LMU.

##### Funding Institutional Members

Funding institutional members provide the OSC with a yearly membership fee to get priority in our training programmes and consultations.

[View Funding Institutional Members](funding-institutional-members.llms.md)

##### Affiliate Institutional Members

Affiliate institutional members share the goals of the OSC and provide support in kind (e.g. infrastructure, services, collaborations).

[View Affiliate Institutional Members](affiliate-institutional-members.llms.md)

### Become an Institutional Member of the OSC

With a statement of commitment to work towards the OSC goals - LMU institutions can become Affiliate Institutional Members. Institutional Members are also encouraged to provide yearly funds to the OSC (depending on the size of the institution) - they then become Funding Institutional Members, which have additional benefits.

[Learn More](becoming-institutional-members.llms.md)
