# Educator Toolkit

##### What does the toolkit offer?

Do you want to **teach** open, responsible, and reproducible research practices? Then our **educator toolkit** is for you! In this toolkit, you can find:

- **Pedagogical materials** and practical **guidance** for effectively **designing and delivering classes and workshops on Open Research topics** to diverse audiences. These materials include core didactic skills, learner engagement strategies, Open Science leadership, and accessibility practices.
- **Ready-to-use materials** for teaching open research practices such as **methodological foundations of Open Science, computational reproducibility, data management and sharing.** This material consist of slides and activity sheets. Slides include presenter notes (intended narrative of the presentation), and further instructor notes (additional guidance intended only for the presenter such as didactic and accessibility tips).

Simply select your intended target audience below and off you go! All materials are grounded in empirical and pedagogical theory. Depending on the topic, materials come in a variety of formats (Quarto slides, HTML, PDF, MS PowerPoint and Google Documents).
