## Mission Statement

Through our Consortiu for Business, Economics and Related Data in the National Research Data Infrastructure (BERD@NFDI), we promote the (re-)usability and sharing of (unstructured) data. The focus of my work is to organize training and educational toolboxes in our [BERD Academy](https://www.berd-nfdi.de/berd-academy/) for researchers to make use of these data, learning the necessary data science methods and data management practices. In BERD@NFDI, we also offer services to make your life with research data easier: For example our [interactive Virtual Assistan](https://www.berd-nfdi.de/legal-questions/)t (iVA) which guides you through the GDPR.  
In my research as a sociologist, I am interested in the description & assessment of competences in data science as well the implementation & evaluation of different (e-)learning formats exploring various data sources.
