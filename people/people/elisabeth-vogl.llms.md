## Mission Statement

I am postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Personality and Educational Psychology at the Department of Psychology.

My passion is to understand emotional processes and how they interact with motivation and cognition particularly in educational contexts. In my opinion, the Open Science Movement is a great reminder for the scientific community that only together we can create reliable knowledge. Therefore, I am committed to implement openness and transparency in my own research, and to encourage it in my reviews, as well as in cooperation with colleagues and students.
