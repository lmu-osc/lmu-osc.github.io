## Mission Statement

In my work as Professor of Empirical Economic Research at the Department of Economics, LMU Munich, I focus on individual decision-making, health economics, and the design and analysis of household surveys. My involvement in the Center reflects my commitment to fostering open scientific inquiry that strengthens evidence-based research, enhances data integrity, and promotes collaborative knowledge exchange.
