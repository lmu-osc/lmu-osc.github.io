## Mission Statement

I am professor for Psychological Methods and Assessment at the Department of Psychology.

Research transparency is a core value of my working group. In my term as managing director of the Department Psychology, I supported the foundation of the department’s Open Science Committee ([osf.io/mgwk8/](https://osf.io/mgwk8/)) in July 2015. Furthermore, many members of my working group signed the „Commitment to Research Transparency and Open Science“ ([www.researchtransparency.org/signatories/](https://www.researchtransparency.org/signatories/)).
