## Mission Statement

I am lecturer at the Geschwister-Scholl-Institut (GSI) for Political Science at the University of Munich (Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität).

For a free society to thrive we need an open discussion of ideas. Our responsibility as scientists is to do our part and be fully open with our procedures, tools and methods. Personally, I am committed to make it easy for others to replicate what I do, as it is the only way to review the procedures, to spot inconsistencies, and to provide new ideas to make science advance.
