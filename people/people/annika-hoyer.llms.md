## Mission Statement

I am a professor for Biostatistics and Medical Biometry at the University of Bielefeld. My research focuses on meta-analyses, compartment models and their application in medicine where I also want to strengthen replicability and reproducibility. I am also part of the reproducibility team of the Biometrical Journal to support transparency in statistical methodology.
