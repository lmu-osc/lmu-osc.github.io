## Mission Statement

I find Open Science important because transparent and reproducible research strengthens both scientific quality and public trust. In my own work, I already value clear documentation, reproducible workflows, and sharing methods and technical insights whenever possible. I am especially interested in improving accessibility of research tools and promoting interdisciplinary collaboration between applied research, data science, and real-world implementation. I see the most important areas of Open Science in reproducibility, open methodologies, and creating research practices that make scientific knowledge easier to verify, reuse, and build upon.
