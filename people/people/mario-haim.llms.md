## Mission Statement

I am professor for Communication Science, especially Computational Communication Research, at the Department of Communication and Media.

While various principles of Open Science are embraced more in some parts of our field, others face severe challenges. The use of third-party data, for example, or working with individuals’ personal data through surveys and in-depth interviews, stands in some contrast to the unlimited sharing of data and materials. In a recent DFG-funded project, we seek to identify such challenges and potential solutions to them.
