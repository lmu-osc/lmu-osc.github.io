## Mission Statement

Openness and transparency are indispensable features of scientific inquiry. If  
taken seriously, the idea of „organized scepticism“ requires researchers to be fully transparent about  
their arguments, data, and methods. In reality, though, too much science is conducted behind  
closed doors. In my research, I embrace open science practices while shedding light on factors that  
lead others (not) to do so.  
My PhD work is part of the DFG priority program [META-REP.](https://www.meta-rep.uni-muenchen.de/index.html) I am passionate about data  
visualization, statistical literacy, and developing software tools that make the adoption of open  
science practices easier.
