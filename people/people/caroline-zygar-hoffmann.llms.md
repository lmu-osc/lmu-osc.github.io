## Mission Statement

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Psychological Methods and Assessment at the Department of Psychology.

My research is at the intersection of motivational psychology, relationship research and psychological methods. As a signatory of the [Commitment to Research Transparency and Open Science](https://www.researchtransparency.org/) I am committed to implement openness and transparency in every step of my own research, and to encourage it in my reviews, as well as in cooperation with colleagues and students.
