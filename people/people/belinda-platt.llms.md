## Mission Statement

I am currently a temporary (“Vertretungs”) Professor of Developmental and Child and Adolescent Clinical Psychology at the University of Kiel. Since 2013 I have been a post-doctoral researcher in the [Department of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry at the LMU Klinikum](https://www.lmu-klinikum.de/kinder-und-jugendpsychiatrie/klinik-kompakt/unser-team/2e8bb7baefad4e9c), where I maintain my research group. Since conducting my PhD I have been passionate about improving transparency in Psychology research. In 2017 I signed the [Commitment to Research Transparency statement](https://www.researchtransparency.org). Since 2021 I have been a member of the [Open Science in Medicine (OSIM)](../../../../meetups/osim/index.llms.md "LMU Open Science Initiative in Medicine"). I try to upload principles of Open Science in my research group.
