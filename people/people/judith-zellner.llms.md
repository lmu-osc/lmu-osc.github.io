## Mission Statement

I am a researcher at Lehrstuhl für Sonderpädagogik im Schwerpunkt Lernen einschließlich inklusiver Pädagogik (Prof. Gebhardt, LMU). I consider it essential to make my research as transparent and accessible as possible. Scientific knowledge is a public good and should contribute to societal development. Therefore, I am committed to providing open access to research outputs, including data and materials, in order to foster transparency, reproducibility, and scientific exchange. At the same time, I aim to translate my work into educational practice. By developing and sharing Open Educational Resources, I seek to support schools with freely available and evidence-based materials.
