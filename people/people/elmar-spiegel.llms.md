## Mission Statement

In my work as a statistical consultant with the Core Facility Bioinformatics and Statistics at Helmholtz Munich, I focus on robust statistical methodology and practical guidance for research data integrity and reproducibility. My involvement in the Center reflects my commitment to fostering reproducible analytical standards, responsible data practices, and the broad dissemination of reliable scientific knowledge.
