#####  Date & Time

#####  Location

#####  Format

We are incredibly excited to announce the third workshop held by the LMU Open Science Initiative in Medicine (OSIM):

**“Publication of clinical studies”**

The workshop will be online, consisting of two lectures followed by an open discussion:

- Dr. Till Bruckner (Founder of [TranspariMED](https://www.transparimed.org/)): “Research waste and evidence distortion in medicine: Missing clinical trial results in Munich and beyond?”
- Dr. Anja Baumgartner (Quality and Study Manager of [Med. Clinic III, LMU Hospital](https://www.lmu-klinikum.de/innere-medizin-3)): “Veröffentlichung der Ergebnisse klinischer Prüfungen - Herausforderungen in der Praxis”
