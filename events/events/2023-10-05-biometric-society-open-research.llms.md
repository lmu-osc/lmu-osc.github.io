# Open Replicable Research by the International Biometric Society

#####  Date & Time

05 Oct - 06 Oct 2023  

#####  Location

Großhadern, Butenandtstr. 5, Room B0.022  

#####  Format

In-Person  

#####  Language

English  

  

We cordially invite you to the **workshop “Open Replicable Research”** on **October 5th and 6th in Munich**. The workshop is organized by the AG “Ethik und Verantwortung” of the [IBS-DR](https://www.biometrische-gesellschaft.de/) in cooperation with the Open Science Center der LMU, the [IBE](https://www.ibe.med.uni-muenchen.de/index.html) and the [Department of Statistics](https://www.stat.lmu.de/en/). We are delighted to welcome [Prof. Dr. Ulrich Dirnagl](https://www.charite.de/service/person/person/address_detail/prof_dr_med_ulrich_dirnagl-1) from Berlin and [Prof. Dr. Ioana Cristea](https://www.dpg.unipd.it/en/en/ioana-alina-cristea) from Padova as invited speakers. **Prof. Cristea** will speak about **“Implementation of data sharing in clinical research: tractable and intractable problems”**, **Prof. Dirnagl** will give a talk on **“Can statistics save preclinical research?”**.

The workshop will cover two half days. On the afternoon of October 5th the topic will be “Data Sharing”, while the morning of October 6th is dedicated to **“Replicability”**. [You can find the program here.](https://www.ibe.med.uni-muenchen.de/services/workshopregistrierung/formular-workshop/program.pdf)

In the context of the workshop, our working group meeting with speaker elections will also take place.

The workshop will take place at Butenandtstr. 5 (München-Großhadern), [you can find the directions here.](https://www.ibe.med.uni-muenchen.de/services/workshopregistrierung/formular-workshop/directions_docx.pdf) **The participation in the workshop is free of charge.**

We are looking forward to welcoming you in Munich in October!

– Sabine Hoffmann und Michael Lauseker for the AG “Ethik und Verantwortung”
