## Overview

In the Love Replications Week, learn how to do reproduction and replication studies yourself, submit your own local or virtual events, and show your love for replications — regardless of the type of research that you do!

The OSC was excited to be a partner in this online event where you learn about doing reproduction and replication studies.

Daily lectures and tutorials to learn how to:

- Do reproduction and replication studies
- Choose and justify replication targets
- Collaborate with students on replication projects
- Write reproducible reports and get certified reproducible results
