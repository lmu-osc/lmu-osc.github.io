# Open Access: Options, Requirements, and Funding Possibilities

#####  Date & Time

20 Jan 2020  

#####  Location

Leopoldstr. 30, Training room S1, ground floor  

#####  Format

In-Person  

#####  Language

English  

  

We are offering a workshop on Open Access addressing LMU researches from all disciplines and of all career levels above PhD on 20 January 2020. The workshop aims at providing orientation on Open Access publications in general, on the available options before, during and after a research project as well as on specific requirements and funding possibilities by different funders.

The LMU Munich Open Science Center, the University Library and the Unit for Research Funding join forces to present you an overview on Open Access in order to enhance transparency and provide you with the necessary tools to fulfil the guidelines of research funders such as DFG and European Commission.

The workshop consists of information and discussion sessions and invites attendees to participate actively. Please spread this information to other interested LMU researchers. The number of participants is limited, slots will be available on a first come, first serve basis
