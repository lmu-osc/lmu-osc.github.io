14:00 CET

LMU Open Science Summer School 2025

The LMU Open Science Center (OSC) is excited to announce the call for applications for its 5th hybrid summer school (OSSS25): deadline July 14! This year's iteration has two tracks to provide participants with two sets of skills. You are invited to apply for one or both tracks. For either track, you can choose to participate either online or in-person.

Hosted by: LMU Open Science Center
